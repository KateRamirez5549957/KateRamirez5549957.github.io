My Dream Destination: Bogota, Colombia

Overview

Bogota is a city in Colombia that is also the capital too. It also has a very vibrant culture and art scene, especially it's street art and very famous museum named the "Gold Museum". Also Colombia was named after a Italian explorer named "Christopher Columbus". Ofcourse you can't forget the Amazing, Tasteful, and Delicous food with the most popular food being "Ajiaco", a hearty chicken and potato soup flavored with the Colombian herb guascas. This is just the beginning of bogota and i hope to visit it one day.

Top Attractions

  • Gold Museum
  • Plaza de Bolivar
  • Botero Museum
  • Catedral Pimada de Colombia
  • Monserrate
